title: Introduction
---
Svix makes it easy to send webhooks from your service while ensuring robust deliverability and a great developer experience for your users. You can think of it as webhooks as a service.

:::tip
You can use the [Svix Play webhook debugger](https://www.svix.com/play/) and the [Svix CLI](https://github.com/svix/svix-cli) to inspect, test and debug your webhooks during development.
:::

This document outlines out to **consume** webhooks sent using Svix. For how to use the Svix API please refer to the [general documentation](/).
